Origami Frog

    DID YOU KNOW?

  • Frogs come in all sorts of colours.
  • Many frogs can jump 20 times their own height.Some can jump even higher!
  • Frogs don’t drink water with their mouths. They “drink” by absorbing water through their skin.

Origami Cicada

    DID YOU KNOW?

  • Cicadas are not related to crickets or locust, but are a separate species of their own.
  • The largest cicada is the Empress Cicada which can grow to 2.8 inches with a 7 to 8 inch wingspan.
  • Cicadas spend most of their young life underground and come up only in their last stages of development.

Origami Panda

    DID YOU KNOW?

  • Giant pandas are only about the size of your palm at the time of birth, and they are completely hairless and helpless.
  • Pandas live between 15–20 years in the wild and more than 30 in captivity.
  • Pandas are big gluttons and they cannot go without eating for 5–6 hours.

Origami Camel

    DID YOU KNOW?

  • When camels walk, they move both legs on one side of their body and then the other.
  • Camels have a double row of very long eyelashes to protect the eye from blowing sand.
  • The lifespan of a camel is around 30 to 35 years old.
